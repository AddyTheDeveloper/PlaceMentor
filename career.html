    Behavioral Interview Scenarios (CO6)

    "Tell me about a time you failed."
    Focus on the learning and recovery, not the failure itself.

    ScenarioBest Approach
    Conflict in TeamExplain how you mediated using data and neutral communication.
    Tight DeadlineShowcase prioritization and time management (Agile mindset).
    Strength / WeaknessWeakness should be real but actionable (e.g., "I sometimes over-focus on details").
    Why should we hire you?Align your unique skills with the company's current challenges/needs.
    Where do you see yourself in 5 years?Focus on growth, skill mastery, and contributing to the organization's goals.
